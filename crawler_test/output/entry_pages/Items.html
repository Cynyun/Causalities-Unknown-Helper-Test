Items



Items are loose objects dropped by most breakable structures, enemies, and traders. Items can be carried around and thrown. Most can be used in the inventory, health panel, or swung.

There are currently 432 items (including inaccessible items) in the Itch.io build.

Item weight





thumb|The bottom of the tooltip description of dressing. Notice the weight tag at the top of the block.

An item's weight determines the cost it imposes on the player's carrying capacity and the containers it can fit in. Weight incentivizes players to make decisions and plan ahead with which items to keep as they encounter more.

Heavier items give more recoil when thrown. Many items lose weight as they lose condition, a trait named scale weight, which linearly goes from full weight at to 0.1u at .Item.cs:Item.totalWeight

Condition



thumb|The top of the tooltip description of dressing. Notice the condition, a colored percentage.

An item's condition determines the amount of time or uses it has, in the context of decay rate and condition loss on use respectively. Most items are destroyed when their condition reaches 0%. Many items can have their condition transferred onto other identical items, done by dragging the item onto another item.

The tooltip's condition changes color as it decreases, from to , , and .

Items sometimes give their full effect when used from the inventory, regardless of their condition. This means that, for example, a Neural Booster at and another Neural Booster at will give the same effects when consumed.

Crafting





Certain items can be combined in the inventory to create new items.

The player can open the crafting menu by pressing C or clicking the button in the bottom left corner of the screen.

Some items have special qualities that make them useful for certain crafting recipes, such as "Hammering" or "Foliage."

Items by category



middle|link= Medical


Medical items recover limb health, treat most physical injuries, and delay death. Proper usage of them causes minimal consequence.

Dropped by medical crates, container crates, drop capsules, and life pod chests.



link= Drugs



Drugs are powerful items that influence the body as a whole. They affect pain recovery and change values such as blood viscosity, radiation, sickness, and mood.

Dropped by medical crates, drop capsules, and life pod chests.



link= Food



Food items are consumables that mainly fill the hunger bar and influence permanent mood.

Dropped by Food box, drop capsules, and life pod chests.



link= Water



Water items are consumables that mainly fill the thirst bar and directly increase permanent mood. Some give energized or caffeine.

Dropped by medical crates, Food box, drop capsules, and life pod chests.



link= Containers



Containers store any amount of items, limited only by their capacities and maximum item weights. Containers other than the Grav-Bag decay while in use (while holding at least one item).

Dropped by container crates, drop capsules, and life pod chests.
