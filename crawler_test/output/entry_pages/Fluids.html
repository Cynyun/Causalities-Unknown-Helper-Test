Fluids

⚠️ Note: Not to be mistaken with Tile fluids

Fluids are substances that can be put in fluid containers.

Dropping a fluid container in a puddle of any Tile Fluid will fill it with the fluid, 200ml of fluid per block of tile fluid.

Fluids can be drank by dragging most fluid containers to the middle of your inventory, same as with eating food, applied (by dragging a fluid container containing the fluid to a limb on the health menu) and injected, by using a handful of special fluid containers that have that ability. (Drag the fluid container to the injection site on the health menu, same as with applying. Some will start the syringe minigame, while others inject a certain predetermined amount.)
