Your Purpose





Players control an Experiment, forced on a mission amongst thousands of other Experiments to obtain a "piece of important experimental technology", which was lost by a "miscalibration error", as a form of behavioral research on the species.

Each run begins with a different specimen, with a randomized height, age, and ID at the top of the health panel.

Thousands have been sent before you, you are not the first.




The experimental technology, or 'cargo', or 'battery', is a very powerful wireless power device.

In Gooey, another game from Orsoniks, the primary reason why every building, lamp, and outlet is powered is due to this device.



World



Prologue





Scav Prototype takes place in a sci-fi world where humanity progressed into an interstellar, spacefaring society.


Not much is directly said about this society, though it is implied that development and money have taken precedence over ethics.


The original Experiment (referred to as specimen), and base of every other Gunsawian species, was a secret experiment of combined Rat, Fox, and Human DNA, via unethical practice.

Despite their human-like behaviour, specimens were treated like animals. Held in labs, the first expies were experimented on, and had their genes cloned.

Humans are intentionally left vague on purpose, as to leave it up for interpretation.

However, it is stated that the companies involved with creating Gunsawians would "rather nuke the entire company and erase all evidence of the bioengineering stuff
than let it get out to the public"


Due to this, the lifespan of an experiment is short, fizzling out at the max age of 40.


You play as a non-lab creation in a non-distant future, created via reproduction from other experiment. You are trained and will eventually be sent to the Grey Planet between the age of 10-28.

It is stated that the majority of Gunsawians are sent to the Grey Planet, and only a select few can live out their lives on Earth.


Upon being sent into the Grey Planet, most experiments are chipped.
  • This chip allows the experiment to see their health by focusing, as well as radiation levels.


However, this also acts as a kill switch, allowing death through specialized radiation.



The Grey Planet



The setting of the game is described as a Grey Planet flourishing with life. The game explores the many diverse layers of this planet.


There are zero humans on the Grey Planet during any of the three games, a time period of 95 years since Causalities: Unknown.

Given the existence of a very easy space travel path to the Grey Planet, Humanity has decided to send any excess junk to the planet. Because of this, the mountains of trash and scrap piles, alongside accidental sends of medical equipment, containers, and everything are found in the current game.

Due to a prior world war on earth, old guns, specialized booby traps and dangers are sent to the Grey Planet to be disposed of, thus forming the traps found in the game.



Ending




Future




The canon ending of Casualties: Unknown has the battery explode, thus breaking.

Another game by Orsoniks, Gunsaw, is set 10-15 [?] years after Casualties: Unknown.

Through natural death, radiation, and potentially another unknown cause, the majority of beings in CU do not live to see the Gunsaw world. The Mercenary is one of the few, due to their body-swapping tech.


In Gunsaw, several different species originating from the Experiment are present, as well as basic civilization and warfare.

These new species were sent by the Company, as they remove every Gunsawian from their labs. During the events of Gooey 78-85 years after CU, there are no more Gunsawians on Earth.

Alongside the new species, the Company sent a incredibly large number of firearms onto the Grey Planet to observe what would happen. This naturally led to war, thus forming the setting for Gunsaw.

On the Grey Planet, however, exist 100-200 thousands of Gunsawians.


In the far future, 70 years after the events of Gunsaw, the same lab company that has created Gunsawians sends Goo monsters for testing to another planet, this one without a breathable atmosphere. During this time, there exists tens to hundreds of millions of Gunsawians on the Grey Planet.

As Earth no longer sends junk and supplies to the Grey Planet and instead dumps it on the alternative planet, the Gunsawian population stabilizes.

During this era, the events of another of Orson's games, Gooey, begins.
In Gooey, you play as a volunteer working for The Company. You control a Goo monster's brain via a specialized chip, and fight off other Goo monsters on this new planet.

It has also been confirmed that goo monsters have been sent to the Grey Planet and interacted with Gunsawians, where Gunsawians have experimented with taking goo monsters down the planet's layers out of curiosity.
