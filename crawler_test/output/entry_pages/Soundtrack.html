Drones

There are a few ambient drones that play when specific conditions are met. Normal ambient music is silenced when a drone is playing.

List of drones
Name File Plays when

Is this the end..?Isthistheend.oggPlays when the player is in dire physical health (bleeding out, starving, severely irradiated, fibrillation progressing, etc).
ExhaustedExhaustedLoop.oggPlays when the player is below 50% stamina. Gets louder the lower stamina is.
TiredTired.oggPlays when the player is below 25% energy. Gets louder the lower energy is.
Bio-hazardBiohazard.oggPlays when the player is currently getting irradiated (chipped only).

Trivia

  • Several songs in this game share leitmotifs with Orsoniks' previous works.
    • Biological Exclusion Zone has a leitmotif from Bio-synthesis from Gooey.
    • Paranormal Threat has a leitmotif from Clear from Gooey.
    • Downwards has a leitmotif from Scratched Steel from Gooey.
    • Six Thousand Feet Under has a leitmotif from Lost Lights from Gooey.
    • Combat in the Cold and Thermal Shock have leitmotifs from Uncertain from Gooey.
  • Most of the bonus tracks are from Orsoniks' previous project, Gooey.
