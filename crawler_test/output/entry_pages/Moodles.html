Moodles

Status effects that describe the player's condition in the game

Not to be confused with Mood, which is a value that describes the player's mental state.

Moodles are statuses that describe the player's condition as they descend through the caves. Various actions and events will make different moodles appear.

Several simply tell you of a need that the player character has, but others have more impact, ranging from being soaked in water, bleeding from a wound, and many other such events.

Moodles that are positive or whose effects don't stem from a negative event (other than Adrenaline from severe injuries, and Last Stand) will be outlined in a rectangular frame instead of circular.

All moodles here are sorted in the way they appear to the player. Moodles near the bottom are considered side moodles, which only appear in the health panel or when the moodle bar is hovered over.
